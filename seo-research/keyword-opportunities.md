# Keyword Opportunities — Crescent Health Partners
**Target Markets:** Sumter, SC (primary) + Columbia, SC (secondary)
**Research Date:** 2026-05-29

---

## Methodology Note

National and regional keyword volume data is based on healthcare industry benchmarks, observed SERP competition, and competitor page structures. Semrush MCP was not available. Hyper-local variants (e.g., "wound care clinic Sumter SC") will show 50–300 MSV in most tools — but the defining fact is that **no independent private competitor has a well-built page targeting these terms**. Crescent can rank against a hospital domain (DR 60+) only by being more specific, more local, and more structured — not by outpowering Prisma's domain authority.

**Important medical content caveat:** All content recommendations are for educational and informational purposes. No page should promise specific treatment outcomes, claim to cure conditions, or constitute medical advice. All AEO content must be written as "Crescent Health Partners' clinical team provides [service]" not "Crescent Health Partners will heal your wound in X days."

---

## Tier Definitions
- **TIER A** — Must-rank. Direct commercial intent. These pages drive appointments.
- **TIER B** — Should-rank. Supporting the authority of Tier A pages; secondary conversion potential.
- **TIER C** — Nice-to-have. AEO wins, long-tail, low-competition topical authority builders.

## Intent Definitions
- **commercial** — User is ready to book/call.
- **informational** — User is researching; AEO target.
- **navigational** — User is looking for Crescent specifically.

## Volume Tiers
- HIGH: 500+ MSV nationally (local share ~2–5%)
- MED: 100–499 MSV
- LOW: 10–99 MSV (local)
- HYPER-LOCAL: <10 MSV (zero competition)
