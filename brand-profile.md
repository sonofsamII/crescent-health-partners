## Service catalog (their wording)

- **Wound Care Specialists** — slow-healing, chronic, diabetic, venous, arterial,
  atypical wounds, burns; physician-supervised; mobile wound care for hospice /
  in-home / nursing facility. Refers to HBOT chambers. Limb salvage focus.
- **DOT Exams (Medical Exam)** — full-time Certified DOT Examiners. Same-day if
  paperwork brought. Truck parking available. "Faster than your local urgent care."
- **Drug Screens (DOT & Non-DOT)** — CLIA lab. Chain of custody, send-out, and
  in-office rapid screens.
- **Wellness Health Snap Shots (Men & Women)** — concierge lab + screening:
  BP / cholesterol / A1C / PSA / height/weight / mental-health questionnaire / labs
  of choice. Gift certificates available. "We don't judge you."
- **Pre-employment Physicals** — same-day. Includes adolescent sports physicals
  (ages 13–17 = $55). Travels to schools by appt for groups.
- **Child Sports Physicals** — ages 13+. Walk-ins welcome. Groups of 5+ on-site.
- **Medical Weight Loss** — GLP-1 / GIP injections + nutrition + behavior change.
  "No extremes. Change your mind, change your body." Free consult.
- **Pregnancy Tests** — in-office + send-out blood. Privacy assured. Has resource list.
- **Paternity / DNA Tests** — in-clinic or courthouse. Fee-based. Strict chain of custody.
- **Flu & COVID Testing** — incl. curbside swab. Mask requested in winter.
- **IV Hydration Therapy** — spa room with TV, aromatherapy, robe/slippers.
  "Even the tricky IVs — second nature to us." Custom "crescent cocktails."
- **TB Skin Tests + TB Blood Test (QuantiFERON Gold)** — employer required tests.
- **Lab Work** — walk-in blood draws without a doctor's prescription. Fees apply.
- **Wellness & Beauty Services** — growing menu. Screening + referral focus.

## Brand identity (visual)

- **Logo:** Word-mark "Crescent Health Partners" — Wayback did NOT archive the
  image file, only the page HTML. Logo file `Untitled-design-77.png` is referenced
  but binary not captured. Need Sam to provide.
- **Self-described brand color signal:** "PINK Partners" — they refer to themselves
  as "the pink partners" repeatedly. So PINK is a brand color, likely as a warm
  feminine accent (both providers are women).
- **Existing site palette (Divi/MediPulse theme):** generic medical blue + white +
  pink accent typical of that template. No reason to inherit it.
- **Recommended palette for v0.1 (warmer, more grounded, signals wound care +
  women-owned + community):**
  - Primary: **Sumter Pink** `#C84A6E` — saturated rose-pink that nods to "pink
    partners" without being saccharine. Reads adult, clinical, warm.
  - Deep tone: **Healing Plum** `#3A1E2A` — for ink, navigation, contrast.
  - Ground: **Linen** `#F7F2EE` — warm cream background, not stark white.
  - Sage accent: **Crescent Sage** `#7C9A86` — for healing / wound-care motif.
    Used sparingly as secondary accent (icons, dividers).
  - Ink: `#1F1419`
  - Muted: `#7A6B70`

- **Typography direction:**
  - Display: **Cormorant Garamond** italic for emphasis ("the gift of *knowing*",
    "we are your *partners*"). Matches the warmth in their copy.
  - Body: **Geist** sans for clinical readability.
  - Mono: **Geist Mono** for hours, phone, lab values, codes.
